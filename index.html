What TruckOmeter Does

Everything in
One Dashboard

We aren't an ELD and we aren't an accounting app — we're the tool that connects the dots between your miles and your money.

📦

Load Tracking

Log pickups, deliveries, rates, and detention time for every load. See exactly where your revenue is coming from.

⛽

Fuel Analysis

Track every fill-up, MPG, DEF usage, and diesel price arbitrage across state lines. Know where to fuel and when.

📊

Real ROI

Driver pay, fuel, expenses, and depreciation — all counted so your net per mile is accurate before you accept a load.

🗺️

IFTA Reports

State-by-state mileage automatically calculated from your load data. Generate your quarterly IFTA report in minutes.

⏱️

Detention Time

Log detention at pickup and delivery. Build a documented history to support detention fee claims with your broker.

🛣️

Route Planning

Segment-aware routing with fuel stop recommendations based on diesel prices and your tank capacity.

🧑‍✈️

Driver Pay

Calculate driver pay per mile or per load — always shown as a real business cost, even if you're the driver.

🔧

Maintenance Log

Track service intervals, costs, and upcoming maintenance per truck and trailer. Stay ahead of breakdowns.
