Delete Account

You can request deletion of your TruckOmeter account and associated data at any time.

1

Send a deletion request

Email us at work.lehp@yahoo.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request. Include the email address associated with your TruckOmeter account.

2

Confirmation

We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days and process the deletion within 30 days.

3

What gets deleted

Your Firebase Authentication account and all associated cloud data will be permanently removed. Local data stored on your device must be uninstalled manually by removing the app.

Data Retention Summary

Firebase account Deleted immediately
Email address Deleted immediately
Firestore backup data Deleted within 30 days
Local device data Removed by uninstalling the app
Community site data Retained — shared community resource, not personal data

Your operational data stays yours. Load records, fuel logs, expenses, and financial data are stored locally on your device. They are never sold or shared with third parties.

Ready to delete your account? Send us an email and we'll take care of the rest. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
